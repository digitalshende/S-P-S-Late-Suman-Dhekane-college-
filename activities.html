Co-Curricular Activities

The co-curricular activities conducted in the institution are in tune with academic (curricular) activities and essentially are student-centric activities.

In our journey as an institution, we have observed that the needs of students and thereby of stakeholders keep on changing over time. We have used different sets of reference points in different time zones and kept the co-curricular activities evolving till date.

Highlights of Co-Curricular Activities in Academic Year:
  • Personality development lectures
  • Industrial visits for students
  • Entrepreneurship development lectures
  • Sports
  • Alumni guidance
  • Various competitions for students to inculcate skills to be good pharmacists
  • Industry experts lectures
  • Institute newsletter
  • Other social activities like blood donation, tree plantation, safety awareness programmes, etc.

Conduction of an activity or its feedback on success or failure is not sufficient enough. We always try to synchronize all the curricular activities in Project Report of Students’ Practical Training (Part III, ER 2020, published by PCI, New Delhi).
