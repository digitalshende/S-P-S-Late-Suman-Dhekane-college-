D. Pharmacy Program Admission Details

Important admission information — Institute Code 6479
Institute Code: 6479
Choice Code (CAP Round): 647982310
Choice Code (CAP Round - TFWS): 647982311 (T)

Eligibility

(1) Maharashtra State Candidature Candidate

  • Candidate should be an Indian National.
  • Pass in 10+2 examination (science academic stream) with Physics, Chemistry and Biology or Mathematics.
  • OR any other qualification approved by the Pharmacy Council of India as equivalent to the above examination.
  • Note: Other than Maharashtra State candidates shall be eligible for Institution quota only.

(2) NRI / OCI / PIO, Children of Indian workers in the Gulf countries, Foreign National

  • Pass in 10+2 examination (science academic stream) with Physics, Chemistry and Biology or Mathematics.
  • OR any other qualification approved by the Pharmacy Council of India as equivalent to the above examination.

Admission Procedure

  • The detailed schedule of admission will be displayed as per DTE instructions.
  • The provisional and final merit list will be displayed on the DTE official Website.
  • Admission rounds are conducted strictly on merit basis.
  • The complete admission process is carried out according to the schedule given by D.T.E., Maharashtra State, Mumbai & guided by Pravesh Niyantran Samiti, Maharashtra State, Mumbai.

Reservation Policy

Reservation policies are implemented as per Government rules and regulations.
